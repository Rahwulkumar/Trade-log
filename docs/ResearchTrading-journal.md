12. Conclusion
Our TradingJournal platform has a solid foundation with unique strengths in prop firm tracking and a distinctive UI aesthetic. However, there are critical feature gaps in:

Trade Import (no broker sync, no CSV upload)
Export Capabilities (no PDF/CSV export)
Advanced Analytics (missing MFE/MAE, hold time, hourly performance)
Backtesting (completely absent)
Customization (no custom dashboards/widgets)
To compete effectively, we should prioritize:

Short-term: CSV import/export, complete Reports page, enhanced tagging
Medium-term: Broker sync, advanced analytics, backtesting
Long-term: AI insights, mobile apps, community features
Our unique prop firm tracking is a differentiator that should be further developed and marketed as a key strength for prop traders.
